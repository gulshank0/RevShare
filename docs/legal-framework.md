# Legal Framework Documentation

## Investment Agreement Template

### Revenue Share Agreement Terms

**IMPORTANT LEGAL DISCLAIMERS:**
- Revenue shares constitute investment contracts and may be considered securities
- Platform operates under SEC exemptions (Regulation D, Rule 506)
- Investments limited to accredited investors only
- All investments carry risk of total loss

### Creator Obligations:
1. **Revenue Transparency** - Monthly YouTube Analytics reporting
2. **Content Compliance** - Adherence to YouTube monetization policies
3. **Investor Communications** - Quarterly performance updates
4. **Channel Maintenance** - Consistent content production requirements

### Investor Rights:
1. **Revenue Distribution** - Monthly payouts based on actual channel earnings
2. **Information Rights** - Access to channel performance metrics
3. **Transfer Rights** - Secondary market trading (subject to restrictions)
4. **Voting Rights** - Limited input on major channel decisions

### Risk Factors:
1. **YouTube Policy Changes** - Platform monetization rule modifications
2. **Creator Performance** - Content quality and audience retention risks
3. **Market Competition** - Industry saturation and competition
4. **Technology Risks** - Platform dependencies and technical failures

### Regulatory Compliance:
1. **Accredited Investor Verification** - Income/net worth requirements
2. **Investment Limits** - Maximum investment caps per investor
3. **Holding Periods** - Minimum investment duration requirements
4. **Reporting Requirements** - Tax and regulatory filing obligations

## Privacy Policy Framework

### Data Collection:
- Personal identification information
- Financial and investment data
- YouTube channel analytics
- Transaction and payment history

### Data Usage:
- Platform functionality and user experience
- Compliance and regulatory requirements
- Investment processing and management
- Communication and customer support

### Data Sharing:
- Third-party service providers (payment processors, KYC providers)
- Regulatory authorities (when required by law)
- Investment counterparties (creators and investors)

### Data Security:
- Industry-standard encryption protocols
- Regular security audits and penetration testing
- Access controls and authentication systems
- Incident response and breach notification procedures

## Terms of Service Essentials

### Platform Usage:
1. **Eligibility Requirements** - Age, location, and accreditation status
2. **Account Registration** - Identity verification and KYC compliance
3. **Prohibited Activities** - Fraud, manipulation, and unauthorized access
4. **Content Standards** - Acceptable use policies for all platform content

### Investment Process:
1. **Due Diligence** - Investor responsibility for research and analysis
2. **Investment Execution** - Order placement and confirmation procedures
3. **Settlement and Custody** - Asset holding and transfer protocols
4. **Fee Structure** - Platform fees, transaction costs, and payment terms

### Dispute Resolution:
1. **Internal Procedures** - Customer support and escalation processes
2. **Mediation Requirements** - Alternative dispute resolution protocols
3. **Arbitration Clauses** - Binding arbitration for legal disputes
4. **Governing Law** - Jurisdiction and applicable legal framework
